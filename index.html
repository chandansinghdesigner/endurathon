Welcome to Endurathon!

Miles of Grit, Moments of Glory.

The roots of Endurathon trace back to the successful organization of ATHM 2023 in Agra, where the passion and energy of runners inspired us to dream bigger. We wanted to take running beyond city limits, into the outskirts of Agra and beyond – to design races that blend challenge, adventure, and natural beauty.
